Console air-conditioner

Console air conditioners are efficient and compact devices that work exceptionally well in residential spaces. With their attractive design, they blend seamlessly into the air-conditioned room. Console air conditioners are an excellent alternative for spaces where installing underfloor heating would be uneconomical. With both upper and lower air supply, they provide a high level of thermal comfort.

CONSOLE AIR CONDITIONER

The K Series

AUX-(12/16)K

Fast and comfortable cooling

The movable air supply louvers evenly distribute air throughout the room, eliminating temperature fluctuations. The efficient fan of the indoor unit ensures that cooled air reaches even the farthest areas of the room quickly.

Two installation methods - greater installation flexibility

Depending on the room layout, interior design requirements, and user preferences, the unit can be installed on the floor directly against the wall or suspended from the ceiling.

Easy installation and SLIM design

Only 230 mm thick

The console unit’s design allows for easy arrangement of the device in the room. Installing the unit against the wall does not require additional construction. The exceptionally small depth of the unit, only 230 mm, allows for its installation even in limited space.

DC Inverter technology

Warranty of high performance

Both the indoor and outdoor units utilise full DC inverter technology, which is highly efficient and energy-saving.


ULTRA-LOW TEMPERATURE HEATING

Powerful heating

High heating performance even in low temperatures. Thanks to the DC inverter compressor, stable operation in ultra-low outdoor temperatures

CONSTANT HEATING

Comfort

Even in low temperatures, console air conditioner operates optimally for a long period of time maintaining a constant temperature level.


SIMILAR TO FLOOR HEATING

Operation similar to floor heating

Air is blown out from the bottom of the device in heating mode, which ensures a faster temperature rise compared to conventional underfloor heating.

WIRELESS CONTROLLER

New desing

Specifically designed for console air conditioners.


COOLING AND HEATING FUNCTIONS

Year-round 2-in-1 device

Heating in winter and cooling in summer.

HIGH HEATING EFFICIENCY

Efficient operation

Compared to other brands of standard air conditioners.


OPERATION AT VARIABLE VOLTAGE

High reliability

Stable operation with a wide voltage range of 150V-260V.

R32 REFRIGERANT

Ecological, highly effective, safe

All AUX air conditioning units of CAC series operate using the newest ecological refrigerant R32, which, comparing to the commonly used R41OA refrigerant, is characterized by higher efficiency and a lower greenhouse effect. It also does not damage the ozone layer.
